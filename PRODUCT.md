# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Friends and small groups playing a private Ukrainian online board game together in a browser.

## Product Purpose

Kupymisto turns property trading, rent, luck, and memes into a fast shared game. The primary job is to start a room quickly, understand the current economy at a glance, and make satisfying strategic decisions with friends.

## Brand Personality

Energetic, Ukrainian, mischievous. The interface should feel like a memorable game-show opening with local meme culture, readable game state, and playful feedback.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, sterile casino styling, noisy unreadable HUDs, manipulative gambling patterns, and animation that hides important game state.

## Design Principles

- Make every important game action feel consequential.
- Use humor as a layer over clear rules, not as a replacement for them.
- Keep shared state obvious: turns, money, ownership, and the final winner must be legible.
- Celebrate starts, cards, money changes, and the finale with purposeful motion and sound.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`, keep text and color contrast readable, and do not rely on color alone for ownership, card type, or financial changes.
